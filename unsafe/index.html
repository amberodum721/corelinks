Sites to Avoid

Warning signs and dangerous patterns to watch out for online.

🚫 Warning Signs of Dangerous Sites

  • Multiple fake "Download" buttons — real one is small and specific
  • Sites asking you to disable your antivirus
  • Sites requiring surveys before downloading
  • Executable files from random file hosts
  • Sites with excessive pop-ups opening new windows
  • Asking for credit card for "free" content
  • Unofficial "cracked" versions of paid software

How to Protect Yourself

  • Install uBlock Origin
  • Scan files at VirusTotal.com
  • Use ProtonVPN before torrenting
  • Use throwaway emails for sign-ups
