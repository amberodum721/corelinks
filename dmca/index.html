DMCA Takedown Notices

If you believe that a link on CoreLinks points to content that infringes your copyright, you may submit a DMCA takedown notice to us. Upon receipt of a valid notice, we will remove the link from our directory promptly.

To file a DMCA takedown, please send an email to sumithrohan244@gmail.com with the following information:

  • Your name and contact information
  • A description of the copyrighted work you claim has been infringed
  • The specific URL(s) on CoreLinks that link to the allegedly infringing content
  • A statement that you have a good faith belief that the use is not authorized
  • A statement that the information in the notice is accurate and that you are the copyright owner or authorized to act on their behalf
  • Your physical or electronic signature
